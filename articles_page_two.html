When Scammers Call Grandma

Older adults have received urgent messages like this from scammers for too long. In 2023, the FBI received reports of around 200 grandparent scams that resulted in the loss of almost $2 million, which is likely low due to underreporting. There have also been high-profile arrests of these scammers recently. The hit movie Thelma begins with the title character receiving a call where a scammer impersonates her grandson’s voice using deepfake AI technology. The “grandson” claims that he was involved in a car accident and that he needs her to send $10,000 to make bail.
